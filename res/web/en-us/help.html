Part 1: Sidebar menu

On the sidebar menu, you can find four sections: Tools, Shapes, Thickness, Colors. Every section has "buttons" that are used to select tools, shapes, thickness or colors that you, the user, wants to use. At the bottom of the window, above the zoom display, there is a button labeled ">", with this button you can show or hide the sidebar menu.

Part 5: Colors

In this section there are two big displays, that show foreground and background color, below them are 20 colors to choose from as foreground or background color. If you click on on of the big displays, a window will open where you can manually enter a color by their hex resemblance.
